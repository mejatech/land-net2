LandNet›Stamp Duty

Confirm stamp duty

Registrar only — complete the land transfer by confirming stamp duty then registering the title.

🔒 Access restricted

Only Land Registrars can confirm stamp duty and register titles.

🏛 Step 1 — Confirm stamp duty paid

Parcel must be in PENDING state. Confirms stamp duty has been paid to KRA. Advances state to STAMP_PAID.

PENDING parcels: check All Parcels for current state.

📜 Step 2 — Register title to new owner

Parcel must be in STAMP_PAID state. Formally registers the title deed to the new owner. Sets state to FREE — transfer complete.

STAMP_PAID parcels: check All Parcels for current state.

🔄 Complete transfer workflow
FREE → Transfer initiated → PENDING → Stamp duty confirmed → STAMP_PAID → Title registered → FREE ✅

All three steps are recorded as separate immutable transactions on the blockchain ledger.

LandNet›Charge

Place a charge

Register a parcel as loan collateral. Bank officers only.

🔒 Access restricted

Only Bank Officers can place charges on parcels.

🏦 Charge request

ℹ️ About charging

A charge locks the parcel as collateral. While CHARGED, the parcel cannot be transferred or charged again by any institution.

⚠️ Smart contract blocks charge if parcel already has an active charge, caution, or pending transfer.

FREE parcels available to charge: check All Parcels for current state.
LandNet›Discharge

Discharge a charge

Remove a charge once the loan is repaid. Only the charging institution can discharge.

🔒 Access restricted

Only Bank Officers can discharge charges.

✅ Discharge request

🔒 Discharge conditions
Parcel state is CHARGED
Must have an active charge
Caller is the charging institution
MSP verified on every request
Loan reference provided
Must match original charge record
CHARGED parcels: check All Parcels for current state.
LandNet›Caution

Caution & caveat

Place or remove a restriction that blocks all transactions on a parcel.

🔒 Access restricted

Only Registrars and Landowners can manage cautions.

⚠️ Caution request
🚫 Effect of caution

Once placed, the smart contract blocks all further transactions — transfers, charges, discharges — until formally removed by the same owner or a registrar.

🔴 Only the owner who placed the caution, or a Land Registrar, can remove it.

CAUTION parcels: check All Parcels for current state.
