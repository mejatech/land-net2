LandNet›Charge

Place a charge

Register a parcel as loan collateral. Bank officers only.

Access restricted

Only Bank Officers can place charges on parcels.

🏦 Charge request

ℹ️ Charge rules

A charge locks the parcel as collateral. Only one charge is allowed per parcel at a time. While CHARGED, no transfers or second charges are permitted.

⚠️ Smart contract blocks if parcel already has a charge, caution, or pending transfer.

FREE parcels available: Block A/004 · Block B/001

LandNet›Caution

Caution & caveat

Place or remove a restriction that blocks all transactions on a parcel.

Access restricted

Only Registrars and Landowners can manage cautions.

⚠️ Caution request
🚫 Effect of caution

Once placed, ALL further transactions are blocked — transfers, charges, discharges — until formally removed by the same owner or a Land Registrar.

🔴 Only the owner who placed the caution, or a Registrar, can remove it. Smart contract verifies caller MSP on every removal.
